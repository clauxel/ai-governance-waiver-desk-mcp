# Evaluation Guide

Use this page to evaluate whether AI Governance Waiver Desk MCP fits a real workflow.

## What To Test

- AI governance waiver MCP
- AI exception approval gate
- AI use case policy MCP
- AI risk waiver receipt

## Expected Evidence

- Submit use case, model, risk level, exception reason, owner, and expiry date.
- Evaluate policy fit, risk class, and required approval path.
- Return allow, review, deny, or expired with structured reasons.
- Archive a waiver receipt and export governance evidence.

## Risk Checks

- Do not use public issues for sensitive use-case details.
- Set expiry dates for every exception.
- Keep reviewer identity public-safe in examples.
